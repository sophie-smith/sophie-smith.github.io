PROJECTS

Dynamic Memory Allocator (July 2018)

Implemented a dynamic memory allocator for Introduction to Computer Systems class (15-213) at Carnegie Mellon University in C. Project included starting with a baseline allocator and optimizing the implementation to increase both throughput and memory utilization.
Also helped students debug their implementations using GDB while serving as a teaching assistant for this class.

Comparison of Social Factors vs. per capita GDP (2016-17)

Analyzed the relationship between per capita GDP of 79 countries and various social parameters, including internet usage, literary rates, etc. using k-means clustering, partitioning and exploratory data analysis in R. Placed 2nd in the Behavioral Science category of the Upstate New York JSHS and competed at the 55th National JSHS
